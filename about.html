The Rising Ethiopia

With the rapid and continuous economic growth for more than ten years, how to rapidly enhance urban landscape and taste has become a very pressing wish for the current Addis Ababa government and even Ethiopian government. Building a multi-functional, high quality urban complex in Addis Ababa as soon as possible will receive welcome and support from all sectors of the society. Under such a background, Poli Lotus International Center has emerged through years elaborate the preparation.

Poli Lotus International Center is located In Addis Ababa the capital city of Ethiopian which, with the population of about 6 million is the political, economic and cultural center of Ethiopia and also where the head quarter of Africa Union is located as well as the most important hub city of aviation in Africa.
